About Me

I am a highly skilled Finance and Business Intelligence Analyst with certifications in FMVA and BIDA. My expertise spans financial modeling, data analysis, and strategic business intelligence, underpinned by a strong technical foundation in aeronautical engineering.

I am a focused, positive, and creative thinker who excels in dynamic environments. Self-motivated, an initiator, and uphold the highest level of professionalism — both as an individual contributor and as a team leader.
